HAVE A FUN RAISER

Fundraising is a great way to get involved and can be done in many different ways. Please click on the blog tab or events box on the home page for a list of fundraising ideas. You can also go to the donate page for the different ways you can help us with donations.

VOLUNTEER

If you live on the Islands of Hawaii and would like to know more about our volunteering opportunities please contact us by clicking on the contact us tab.

CORPORATE SUPPORT

Corporate tax deductible grants are a great way to help You Are Loved Inc, get the home medical supplies and equipment we need. We also need funds to hire our health care professionals to care for those with health issues and mental disorders. You can also support our mission by helping us put our respected veterans and those with disabilities to work.

SHARING

Sharing our story and mission with others is a great way to help get the word out on our organization. Like us and invite others to our Facebook page, our page is open for anyone to share a story with us. We also will be posting upcoming events and fundraisers we will be doing and ways you can help.
