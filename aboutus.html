ABOUT US

You Are Loved, Inc. has been formed to provide aid to veterans, the elderly and those with disabilities throughout the Hawaiian Islands but we will not be limited to just Hawaii and hope to expand our services to those in need throughout the US and abroad in the near future. Our services will be providing employment opportunities within the community through special programs to inform companies of the experience and willingness to work of our selfless Veterans and those with disabilities. For our Veterans we will provide and also hire health professionals to specifically deal with the suffering from physical and mental issues of PTSD. We are here to assist the elderly and disabled that have trouble leaving their homes by providing and hiring caregivers to go into the home to help with personal hygiene, cooking, cleaning, transportation, laundry and other basic needs.

 Our beloved elderly and disabled participants may also be presented with medical assistance within the home for non-emergency procedures such as taking vital signs, administering medication, drawing blood etc.… All of the participants for our program may also receive food, hygiene items, and cleaning supplies to help with the needs that align with our mission.   I started this organization with all the unconditional love I have deep within my soul for humanity. God has been guiding me all my life and I always knew deep within that caring and helping those in need is and always will be my purpose in this life.
